Burn Voting -

 Introduction - 

Burn voting enables users to burn STKHB to change the state of contract variables. This includes reward block times, reward rates & minimum vote amounts. Burn voting is accessable to anyone who holds STKHB. Minimum vote thresholds are set for burn votes and all STKHB voted is immdieatly and permanently burned. Voted STKHB is non-recoverable.  

To view current proposals and vote on them please visit our Burn Voting Center.
