How to Add Liquidity to STKHB

Adding liquidity to STKHB markets is a great way to support the project, increase market stability & potentially increase your earnings over HODLing alone. Before adding liquidity to STKHB markets, familirize yourself with the risks and DYOR into liquidity provision. 

1. Navigate to -
 Coming Soon!

1-1. You may be prompted when you first land on the page to accept adding STKHB as a token. Check the box to accept and continue to the next screen.
 
1-2. If you have not yet connected your Web3 wallet to PancakeSwap, you will be prompted to do so instead of seeing the display below. 

1-3. If you navigate to PancakeSwap's homepage instead of the link above, click "Trade" then "Liquidity".

2. Supply Liquidity - 

2-1. Enter the amount of either STKHB or BNB that you would like to add to the liquidity pool. You will need equal amounts of STKHB and BNB to provide liquidity.

2-2. Click "Approve STKHB" and sign in to your Web3 wallet pop-up. It may take a few moments for "Supply" to no longer be greyed out while the transaction is approved.

2-3. Click "Supply" and approve the transaction in your Web3 wallet. After the transaction is complete, you will be able to see your liquidity token statistics by clicking "Trade" then "Liquidity" and selecting the drop down menu on "STKHB/BNB".
