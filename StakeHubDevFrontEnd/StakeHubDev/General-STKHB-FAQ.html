General StakeHub FAQ

We cover some basic FAQ here. If you have questions and we have not provided an answer here, join us in discord and well help you as soon as possible.

StakeHub FAQ -

What is StakeHub? -

StakeHub is a fixed APY, no principal risk staking platform. For detailed information on what StakeHub does, please visit  StakeHub Introduction .

What is STKHB? -

STKHB is the official token of StakeHub, created by our  StakeHub Contract . STKHB is rewarded for staking on the StakeHub platform. 

How can I stake STKHB? -

STKHB holders can stake on the StakeHub application. Please visit  How to Stake STKHB  for more detailed information. 

What is staking? - 

Staking is the process of providing proof of ownership over tokens and coins to earn rewards. By staking tokens on StakeHub, users are rewarded with STKHB tokens.

Will StakeHub open more staking pairs? -

Yes. We currently offer staking services for STKHB but plan to release contracts for many other crypto assets. Please visit our  roadmap  and  discord  for updates on soon to be released staking pairs. 

Why is my BNB balance zero? -

The two main causes for a zero BNB balance are not being connected to the Binance Smart Chain, or not having BNB on the Binance Smart Chain. Double check that your wallet network is set to "BSC" and double check that your BNB balance is BEP20 instead of BEP2. If you need to swap from BEP2 to BEP20, there are many resources available to you depending on the wallet you use.    

What is the max STKHB supply? -

There is no maximum STKHB supply. Supply is controlled by built-in and user driven burn events.

What is the transaction fee to interact with STKHB? - 

STKHB was created on the Binance Smart Chain. Every time you interact with the token (staking, redeeming, etc.), you will have to pay a Binance Smart Chain fee in BNB. Additionally, there is a 0.5% fee to create new stakes.
