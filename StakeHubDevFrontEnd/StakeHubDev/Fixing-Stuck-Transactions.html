Fixing Stuck "Pending" Transactions

If a transaction has been stuck as "pending", the gas price you selected may not be sufficient  for current network conditions. When a wallet has "stuck" transactions, it cannot function properly until they are cleared. We have put together a few methods to assist you with clearing this and getting you back in action.

Method 1 - Patience


If it is not urgent that you unstick your transaction, or you do not feel comfortable overriding the transaction, patience is the best option for you.

If you have a transaction that has been stuck as "pending" for more than a few minutes on the Binance Smart Chain, chances are it will not go through. The easiest method to clear these transactions is to wait for them to expire. This can take anywhere from 15 minutes to one hour depending on the amount of transactions of you have stuck as well as network conditions.

Methodology -

Wait. Check your Web3 wallets pending transactions periodically to see if the pending transactions switch to a "failed" state. You will need to wait for the entire backlog of "pending" transactions to clear before you resubmit a transaction to be accepted by the blockchain. Submitting new transactions that do not override the current transaction prior to clearing your backlog will only extend the amount of time you must wait. 

Prevention -

If you are experiencing a large number of "stuck" transactions, your gas limits may be set too low for current market conditions. Setting custom limits for gas and increasing the GWEI a few points will increase the priority of your transaction by increasing the fee you pay. For instructions on increasing gas limits, please refer to Method 2.

Method 2 - Override "Stuck" Transactions with a New Transaction Using the Same Transaction Nonce & Higher Gas Limits


This may sound daunting but it is the fastest method to clear pending transactions.

Methodology -

Step 1 - Enable Custom Gas & Nonce Controls on your Web3 Wallet

For this guide we will be using Metamask. 

1-1 - 

Open your Metamask plugin & locate the account icon in the top right corner (circle icon with default art). Click the account icon and select "Settings" from the menu that appears. 

1-2  - 

Locate and click "Advanced" in the menu that appears after clicking "Settings". 

Scroll down and enable "Advanced gas controls" in the menu that appears after clicking "Advanced".

Scroll down further and enable "Custom transaction nonce".

Step 2 - Locate "Stuck" Transaction Nonce

In order to submit a transaction to override the stuck one, we first have to figure out what nonce to use.

2-1 -

If you still have "Settings" open, head back to your wallet display that shows your token balances.

Select the token with the "stuck" transaction.

2-2 -

Locate and click the "pending" transaction in the token menu.

After clicking the "pending" transaction, locate and record the "transaction nonce".

Step 3 - Override Your "Stuck" Transaction

Overriding our previously stuck transaction, we must submit a new transaction using the same transaction nonce. Once the new transaction is accepted by the network, your old transaction will be cancelled. 

3-1 -

Create a new transaction. Locate the gas section labelled "GWEI".

Increase the "GWEI" value from the default value. Typically, you will want to double the preset "GWEI" to unstick a transaction. Confirm that your changes have reflected in the transaction confirmation screen. Do not submit your transaction yet. 

3-2 - 

After changing the "GWEI" value, locate "Custom nonce" in the transaction window. 

Change the "Custom nonce" to the same nonce value as your stuck transaction. 

Submit the transaction. 

Overview -

After submitting a replacement transaction with a higher GWEI value, the transaction should be picked up by the network and approved quickly on the Binance Smart Chain. If your replacement transaction is stuck pending as well, resubmit a new transaction with an even higher GWEI value until the transaction is approved.
