Non-Burn Voting

Non-burn voting enables users to vote on non-state changes of the STKHB contract. This includes adding or removing staking pairs, burn events & mechanisms, community events & more.

Non-burn voting is a simple yes or no to the current proposal. The value of a users vote is based on the size of their stake. Users who do not currently hold a stake are not able to vote in non-burn proposals.

Burn Voting

Burn voting enables users to vote on state changes of the STKHB contract. This includes changing reward block times & reward rates (APY for contracts), minimum vote amounts, & more.

Burn voting requires a user to spend STKHB to vote. Vote amounts are based on the amount of STKHB a user burns to vote yes or no to a proposal. STKHB used to vote is permanently burned and is non-recoverable.
